VIEW

CASE STUDIES

Learning Carnival and its associates have resolved hundreds of problems using learning solutions for a diverse range of organisational issues. Here are some cases where we have achieved exceptional results.

Employee Onboarding:

A large global technology company faced challenges in retaining and engaging employees, particularly new recruits. Learning Carnival created a proficient onboarding process by developing an impactful program that not only conveyed the organisation's culture but also helped new hires become acquainted with their colleagues, understand their KRAs, and feel confident about their work.

Legal & Compliance:

An insurance company faced severe compliance issues, receiving multiple warnings from regulatory bodies. Our learning solutions conveyed crucial information through real-world scenario simulations, aiding employees in understanding complex legal concepts and compliance requirements. This approach fostered emotional connection, instilling a heightened sense of seriousness among employees about the situation.

Product Training:

A heavy engineering firm, specializing in dewatering solutions, sought to redirect its sales and dealer teams toward solution-focused selling. Our devised blended solution, integrating case studies, role plays, simulations, demo videos, and group discussions, delved into product specifics, illustrating how the offering resolved real-life customer issues. The outcome was a notable surge in high-ticket solution sales, eclipsing lower-ticket product sales, underscoring the success of our implemented training program.

Sales Enablement Training:

A packaging company aimed to transition from its current state to a packaging products company, extending its new product line to both businesses and direct customers. However, the sales team was initially unprepared for the shift to the B2C market. Learning Carnival played a pivotal role in altering the sales team's perspective, offering reasons for transformation and emphasizing the urgent need for change. Additionally, we crafted various sales collaterals to bolster their confidence as they entered the market.

Diversity, Equality & Inclusion:

A progressive Indian company hired individuals with disabilities for simpler tasks, supporting NGOs in integrating their members into the mainstream. Recognizing potential communication challenges and discomfort among staff, a sensitization program was initiated. Learning Carnival conducted role plays to foster empathy, shedding light on the challenges faced by individuals with disabilities. This approach led to a more inclusive and welcoming environment, ensuring support for those with disabilities when needed, marking a positive impact on workplace culture.

Customer Service Training:

A sports equipment company received customer backlash due to certain service agents failing to communicate altered warranty terms. Learning Carnival intervened, training service representatives in empathetic communication, providing a performance support system for challenging situations, and imparting skills in effectively escalating cases. This learning solution facilitated a win-win scenario for customers, the company, and the service representatives, ensuring improved communication and customer satisfaction.
